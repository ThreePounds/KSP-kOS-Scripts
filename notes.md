Button live cycle:

1. STYLE:NORMAL
2. SYTLE:HOVER - when mouse hovers over
3. STYLE:ACTIVE - when button is pressed
4. STYLE:FOCUSED_ON - before button press is taken
5. STYLE:FOCUSED - after button press is taken
5. STYLE:ON / STYLE:NORMAL_ON - when window is defocused before press is taken
6. STYLE:NORMAL - when window is defocused
